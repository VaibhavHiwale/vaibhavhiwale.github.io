Projects

Tear-Drop Dimple Effects on Elliptical Tube Internal Cooling: Numerical Analysis - MS (Research) Thesis

  •  Numerical Analysis of the Effect of Turbulence on Heat Transfer in an elliptical pipe flow.
  • Implemented Tear Drop Dimples on the surface to increment the heat transfer rate.
  • Used Ansys Fluent to solve the Steady and Unsteady k-Omega SST Turbulence Model to predict the fluid flow and temperature over the turbulent generators.
  • Studies were carried out at various geometric parameters with Reynolds number ranging from 5,000 to 30,000.
  • A 75% increment in Nusselt number and a maximum PEC of 1.1 is achieved.

Solar Based Smart Energy Systems - Traffic Density Control - BE Project

  • The system uses an AT89S52 microcontroller-based development board and IR sensors for vehicle detection.
  • Responsible for developing an efficient algorithm for the system and implementing it using C++ Code on the AT89S52 microcontroller.
  • A fully autonomous miniature-sized working model was fabricated.
