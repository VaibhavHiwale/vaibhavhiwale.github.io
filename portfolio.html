Portfolio

MS Thesis · Best Paper Award
Heat Transfer Enhancement in Elliptical Tube with Teardrop Protrusions

Numerical analysis of turbulence effects on heat transfer in an elliptical tube enhanced with teardrop-shaped protrusions. Implemented unsteady k-ω SST turbulence model in Ansys Fluent. Achieved a 75% increase in Nusselt number and a maximum PEC of 1.14 across Reynolds numbers 5,000–30,000.

Published at HTFF-2023, London · Under review at ASME J. Thermal Sci. Eng. Appl., 2026.

Ansys Fluent k-ω SST Heat Transfer Turbulence

Numerical Study of Pool Boiling

Studied bubble nucleation during pool boiling at varying wall superheat temperatures. Implemented the VOF multiphase model coupled with a Standard k-ε viscous model and enhanced wall treatment to capture bubble growth and departure behaviour.

Built two CFD solvers from scratch. A C implementation of the lid-driven cavity problem using streamfunction-vorticity formulation, and a MATLAB solver for turbulent channel flow using Prandtl's one-equation turbulence model. Both validated against benchmark data.
